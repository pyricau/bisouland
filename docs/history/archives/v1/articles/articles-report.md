# BisouLand (v1) History

Based on the 31 articles from February 2006 to May 2012, here is the timeline and history of BisouLand:

---

## 2006: The Beginning

### February 2006 - Launch

- **Feb 20**: BisouLand launches with a "Rose bonbon" (pink candy) design by Piwa誰
- **Feb 22**: First development phase completes with core mechanics: hearts (generate love points), mouths (construct kisses), and 3 types of kisses (Smack, Baiser, Baiser langoureux)
- **Feb 24-28**: Bug fixes, including exploits where refreshing could restart constructions

### March 2006 - Core Gameplay Expansion

- **Mar 5-6**: Map system introduced - players placed on "clouds" (nuages) with 16 spots each; kisses used to attack neighbors
- **Mar 7**: Full tech tree revealed: Organs (Heart, Mouth, Tongue, Teeth, Legs), Kisses, and Techniques (Apnea, Surprise, Spit, Jump)
- **Mar 9**: Static technology tree for players to plan progression
- **Mar 10**: Roadmap announced: scoring, rankings, attack system, cloud movement

### April 2006 - Game Balancing

- **Apr 26**: Construction times reworked with exponential curve (level 50 heart = 7 days); simultaneous development and attacking enabled
- **Apr 29**: Player data reset after rule changes (max level 20 heart, 10M points, 2000 score)
- **Apr 30**: New design contributed by Dorian

### May-June 2006 - Slowing Down

- **May 14**: Development pauses for admin's university exams
- **June 16**: Chat fixes, Top 20 leaderboard, personalized banners introduced

### August-September 2006 - Version 2 Announcement

- **Aug 8**: BisouLand v2 announced - complete code rewrite from scratch for cleaner architecture and admin tools
- **Aug 10**: Preview available at bisoutest.piwai.info
- **Aug 17**: Piwa誰 turns 20
- **Sep 7**: Admin enters INSA de Rouen engineering school, development slows significantly

---

## 2007-2008: Maintenance Mode

### March 2007

- **Mar 29**: V2 development stalled due to studies; v1 remains operational indefinitely

### February 2008

- **Feb 21**: Minor bug fixes, improved statistics page; Piwa誰 confirms BisouLand will continue running "for years to come"

---

## 2011: Community Takeover

### May 2011

- **May 29**: BisouLand transferred to community management by three players: **ZEAS**, **facepalm**, and **cat_loic**
- Code becomes **Open Source**
- Promise of gradual improvements rather than disruptive changes

---

## 2012: Version 2 Finally Arrives

### May 2012

- **May 2**: **v2.0.0 released** - complete rebuild using **Symfony2** and **Skeleton CSS**
- **May 14**: **v2.1.0 released** - Population feature, new responsive design, automated love point decay system

### Version 1 Final Statistics

- 64 clouds
- 2,415 members
- 1,413,695,139,948 score points
- 2,340,853,121,625.77 PA (Points d'Amour)

---

## Key Figures

- **Piwa誰** - Original creator and developer (2005-2008)
- **Dorian** - Contributed design in April 2006
- **cat_loic** - Long-time player who reported bugs, later became part of new admin team
- **ZEAS, facepalm, cat_loic** - Community members who took over and built v2
